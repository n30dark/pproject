For healthy teeth and a white smile

To help you take good care of your teeth, we’ve developed a range of electrical toothbrushes equipped with Philips’ unique Sonicare technology. These brushes drive fluids between your teeth and along the gum line for healthy teeth and a white smile.

Brush heads subscription service

We want to make sure you get the most out of your Philips Sonicare toothbrush. In order to do so, it’s important to replace your brush every three months. A fresh brush maximizes the effectiveness of Philips’ unique Sonicare technology, so choose the right brush, subscribe and take advantage of the following benefits:

  • Never run out of brushes
  • Delivery to your doorstep
  • Easy to change delivery frequency
  • No commitment
